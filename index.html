To-Do List

Welcome to your To-Do list ...

You can find the instruction of usage below:
To add a task, type in your task and hit "enter" key.
To checkmark a task, click the checkbox on the right-end of each task.
To delete a task, hover your mouse to left-start of the task, then click on the recycle bin.

You can also...

  • Click on the "-" and "+" signs on the right-end of the title to hide/show the input.
  • And finally, use the color wheel on the bottom-right of the page to change the theme of your list!
